Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2018, Undaunted Spirits began with a clear purpose: to create equal opportunities and champion the rights of persons with disabilities across Ternopil and beyond.

Since 2022, as war changed everything for our nation, we answered a new call. We expanded our reach to support veterans, displaced families, and all those whose lives have been disrupted by conflict. Our mission grew, but our spirit remained the same‚Äîundaunted, caring, and community-driven.

Today, we provide essential support ranging from medical supplies and rehabilitation equipment to food, sanitary products, and emotional support. We are a community that refuses to be defeated, no matter the challenge.

Our Partners

Undaunted Spirits works closely with local communities and institutions to make sure assistance reaches the people who need it most. Here are some of the partners who help us deliver impact on the ground.

Communal Institution "Center for the Provision of Social Services" of the Tovste Settlement Council

üèõÔ∏è Municipal Social Services Center üìç Tovste, Ternopil Oblast, Ukraine

A local government social services center serving the Tovste community. The Center supports elderly residents, people with disabilities, large families, and other socially vulnerable groups through social work, care, and targeted assistance.

Our Partnership

Undaunted Spirits collaborates with this Center to identify and support the most vulnerable residents of the Tovste community. The Center publicly thanked Undaunted Spirits / NGO "Unconquered in Spirit" for humanitarian aid including clothing, footwear, and hygiene products delivered to pensioners, large families, people with disabilities, and other socially disadvantaged residents.

"We deeply value our partnership with the Tovste social services team, whose daily work directly improves the lives of people in their community."
