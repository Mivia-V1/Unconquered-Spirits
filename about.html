Our Story

2018

The Beginning

Founded in Ternopil on December 22, 2018, Undaunted Spirits began with a clear purpose: to create equal opportunities and champion the rights of persons with disabilities across our region.

Co-founded by Svetlana Bychkovska and Lesya Rakocha, our focus was social protection, rehabilitation, and integration of people with disabilities into Ukrainian society.

2022

Expanding Our Mission

When Russia invaded Ukraine in February 2022, we answered a new call. Our mission expanded to support all those affected by war‚Äîveterans, displaced families, frontline defenders, and communities in crisis.

We began delivering medical supplies to the front lines, supporting veteran rehabilitation, and providing essential supplies to displaced families‚Äîall while maintaining our original disability support work.

Today

Serving Multiple Communities

Today we provide comprehensive support: medical supplies, rehabilitation equipment, essential living supplies, veteran care, and continued advocacy for persons with disabilities.

7 years of continuous operation. 200+ active members. 1,500+ people directly supported. 4 major program areas. $0 administrative overhead.

Our Mission

To empower and support persons with disabilities and all those affected by hardship in Ukraine, creating equal opportunities and championing dignity for every person we serve.

Our Vision

A Ukraine where every person, regardless of ability or circumstance, has the opportunity to live with dignity, independence, and hope for the future.
