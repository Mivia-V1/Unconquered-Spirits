Meet Svetlana

Svetlana Bychkovska has led Undaunted Spirits since 2018.
She lives in Ternopil and knows every family we help.

Our Story

2018

We started in Ternopil with one goal: help people with disabilities live with dignity. Svetlana Bychkovska and Lesya Rakocha founded the organization on December 22, 2018.

2022

When war came, we expanded. Veterans needed wheelchairs. Displaced families needed food. Soldiers needed medical supplies. We delivered.

Today

200+ volunteers. 10,000+ people helped. Still 100% volunteer-run. Still $0 in admin costs. Still hand-delivering every item ourselves.

100%

of your donation goes directly to aid.

No salaries. No office rent. No middlemen.
